# Directive: Marketing Strategy v2.0 (The Pivot)

> **CFO NOTE:** This strategy replaces the "Generic Email" approach which had a 0% conversion rate. The focus is now on **High-Effort, High-Conversion** tactics. We are not selling a widget; we are selling a "Modernization Package" (Website + Widget).

## Protocol 1: The Trojan Horse (Website First)
**Logic:** Clinic owners ignore "technical" pitches but react emotionally to their own brand/website.
**Trigger:** Identifying a clinic with an outdated, non-mobile-friendly website (Tier A).

1.  **Preparation:**
    - Do NOT send a generic email.
    - Generate a "Premium Mockup"[^1] of their site using our template.
    - Screenshot the mockup on a mobile frame (iPhone).
2.  **The Hook:**
    - Subject: "Jautājums par [Klīnikas Nosaukums] pacientu plūsmu" (Question about patient flow) OR "Esat pazaudējuši pacientus?" (Are you losing patients?)
    - Body: "I noticed your site is hard to use on iPhone. I took 10 mins to mock up a modern version. Look at the difference."
    - **The Widget:** "I also added a 24/7 booking widget (that's the real magic). It's built-in."

[^1]: Uses `marketing/templates/modern_dental_site.html`

## Protocol 2: The Phone Screen (Bypassing the Gatekeeper)
**Logic:** Admins delete emails. We must speak to the **Owner** or **Head Doctor**.
**Trigger:** Sending the Trojan Horse email (Protocol 1). Call 1 hour later.

**Script:**
- **Me:** "Labdien, es necenšos neko pārdot, man ir tehnisks jautājums par mājaslapu. Es redzu, ka jums ir pieteikuma forma—vai tā bieži nestrādā? Jo es mēģināju..."
- **Admin:** "Ko jūs gribat?" / "Mums viss strādā."
- **Me (Pivot):** "Sapratu. Es tieši šo problēmu risinu klīnikai 'Nadora'. Vai varu nosūtīt video ārstam [Owner Name], kā mēs to salabojām? Tas ir par pacientu plūsmu."

## Protocol 3: The Physical Drop-in (Guerilla)
**Logic:** Harder to ignore a human face. Use only for Tier A clinics in Riga Center.
**Trigger:** No response to Phone/Email after 3 days.

**Script:**
- Walk in. Look like a developer (clean, smart casual), not a salesperson (suits).
- **Me:** "Sveiki, esmu kaimiņš/programmētājs. Es redzēju, ka jums visu laiku zvana telefons. Es uztaisīju rīku, kas to samazina par 50%. Vai varu atstāt savu vizītkarti dakterim?"
- Leave a physical card/flyer with a QR code to the "Trojan Horse" mockup of *their* specific clinic.

## Protocol 4: The "Nadora" Leverage
- **Rule:** Every pitch must mention "Nadora" once they are signed.
- **Goal:** Social Proof. "Nadora uses this."

## Anti-Patterns (What We DO NOT Do)
- **NO** sending to `info@` without a specific Owner Name target.
- **NO** selling "Automation" or "AI". Sell "More Patients" or "Less Phone Calls".
- **NO** generic "Dear Sir/Madam".
