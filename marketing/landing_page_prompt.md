# Landing Page Generation Prompt

**Role:** Expert UI/UX Designer & Web Developer
**Context:** creating a high-converting landing page for a B2B SaaS product called "Clinic Automation".
**Target Audience:** Dental clinics and small medical practices in Latvia.
**Goal:** Persuade clinic owners to start a free trial by showing how automation saves time and money.

---

## Visual Aesthetic & Style
*   **Vibe:** Professional, Trustworthy, Clean, Modern, "Medical Tech".
*   **Color Palette:**
    *   Primary: Deep Medical Blue (#0055ff or similar) - evoking trust.
    *   Secondary: Calming Mint/Teal (#00d2d3) - evoking health and peace.
    *   Background: Clean White (#ffffff) and very light gray (#f8f9fa) sections to separate content.
    *   Text: Dark Slate (#2d3436) for readability, never pure black.
*   **Typography:** Modern Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter, Roboto, or Lato). Clean, readable at a glance.
*   **Imagery:** High-quality images of happy receptionists, clean clinic interiors, or abstract "scheduling/calendar" 3D illustrations. Glassmorphism effects on cards.

## Page Structure & Content

### 1. Hero Section
*   **Headline:** "Automatizēta pacientu plūsma Jūsu klīnikai"
*   **Subheadline:** "Samaziniet neierašanos skaitu un atbrīvojiet administratorus no liekiem zvaniem."
*   **CTA Button:** Primary color, big. Text: "Sākt 14 dienu bezmaksas izmēģinājumu"
*   **Visual:** A dashboard mockup showing a calendar getting filled automatically, or a happy doctor.

### 2. The Problem (Pain Points)
*   **Layout:** 3 columns or horizontal cards.
*   **Content:**
    *   Icon: Phone ringing constantly. Text: "Administratori pārslogoti ar zvaniem."
    *   Icon: Empty chair/clock. Text: "Pacienti aizmirst un neatnāk (No-shows)."
    *   Icon: Messy paper planner. Text: "Manuālas kļūdas pierakstos."

### 3. The Solution (Features)
*   **Style:** Alternating layout (Text Left/Image Right, then swap).
*   **Feature 1:** "Automātiski SMS atgādinājumi" - Show an iPhone screen with an SMS reminder.
*   **Feature 2:** "Gudrā gaidīšanas rinda" - Show a slot being filled automatically.
*   **Feature 3:** "24/7 Online Widget" - Show the booking widget floating on a website.

### 4. Interactive Demo / Video Section
*   **Headline:** "Kā tas darbojas 2 minūtēs"
*   **Element:** A large video placeholder or an interactive "Try the Widget" frame.

### 5. Pricing
*   **Layout:** Single simple card (emphasizing simplicity).
*   **Price:** €99 / mēnesī
*   **Features List:** (Checkmarks)
    *   Neierobežoti SMS
    *   Visi ārstu kalendāri
    *   Tehniskais atbalsts
    *   Beztermiņa līgums

### 6. Final CTA (Footer)
*   **Text:** "Gatavi sakārtot savu klīniku?"
*   **Button:** "Pieteikties Izmēģinājumam" (Secondary style)

---

## Technical Constraints for AI Generator
*   Use semantic HTML5.
*   Use TailwindCSS for styling if possible (or clean vanilla CSS).
*   Ensure the design is fully responsive (Mobile-first).
*   Include placeholders for images (e.g., `https://placehold.co/600x400`).
