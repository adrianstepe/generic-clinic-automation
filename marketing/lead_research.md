# Lead Research - Dental Clinics Latvia

## Lead Scoring Criteria

### Tier A (Hot Leads) - Contact First
- Has a website with online booking button that doesn't work well
- Multiple doctors/locations (more complex scheduling = more pain)
- Active on social media (tech-savvy, open to tools)
- Reviews mention "hard to reach" or "long wait times"

### Tier B (Warm Leads) - Contact Second
- Has a website but no online booking
- Single location, 2-3 doctors
- No recent negative reviews about scheduling

### Tier C (Ignore for Now)
- Very small (solo practitioner)
- No website at all
- Already using major PMS with integrated booking (e.g., Dentally, Planmeca)
