# Lead Research - Dental Clinics Latvia

## Lead Scoring Criteria

### Tier A (Start Here) - 3-5 Doctors
- Lower risk if something breaks
- Easier to onboard manually
- Perfect for stress-testing your system

### Tier B (After System Proven) - 6-10 Doctors
- More complex scheduling needs
- Higher value contracts
- Message after 2-3 successful onboardings

### Tier C (Scale Phase) - 10+ Doctors
- Enterprise-level complexity
- Highest revenue potential
- Wait until widget integration is solid
