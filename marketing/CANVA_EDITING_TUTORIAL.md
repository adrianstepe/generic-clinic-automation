**Why Canva?** It's often easier to "Crop" out black bars and add text without technical glitches.

**Goal:** A clean 60-second video for email.

---

## 1. Setup & Upload
1.  Go to [Canva.com](https://www.canva.com) and click **"Create a design"**.
2.  Search for **"Video"** (1920x1080).
3.  **Upload** your screen recording file to the **Uploads** tab on the left.
4.  **Drag** your video onto the white canvas.

---

## 2. The "Speed" & Cuts (Keep it Fast)
Canva has a simple video editor timeline at the bottom.

### Cutting the Boring Parts
1.  Move the playhead line to where you want to cut (e.g., waiting for a page load).
2.  **Right-click** on the timeline strip -> **Split Page** (or press `S`).
3.  Move to where the action starts again -> **Split Page** (`S`).
4.  Click the middle section (the boring part) and press **Delete**.

### Speeding Up (Typing)
1.  Click the clip section where you are typing.
2.  Click **Playback** (top menu bar) -> **Video Speed**.
3.  Set to **2x** or **2.5x**.
